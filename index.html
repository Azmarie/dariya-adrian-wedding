Afternoon

To join us for the ceremony, we would appreciate your arrival by 3 PM.

The outdoor ceremony will unfold against the breathtaking backdrop of scenic mountains and Golden Eagle Golf Club.

We're hoping to get photos with all our amazing guests after the ceremony. Following this delightful photo session, it's straight into the good times - sip, savor, and socialize during our cocktail hour!

Get ready for some fun! We'll kick off with exciting games, and once the laughter and friendly competition wind down, the dance floor will be all yours. Let's celebrate together!
