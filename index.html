Dariya's Story

Dariya was born and raised in Russia, spending much of her childhood in Khabarovsk and Petropavlovsk-Kamchatsky. She studied and worked in Vancouver, Canada, where she met Adrian, her future husband. In her spare time she loves to hang out with friends, eat Popeyes and watch TV with Adrian, and spend time doing different sports.

Adrian's Story

Adrian was born in Ontario, Canada and raised in Hong-Kong. He studies in Vancouver before spending a few years working in Hong-Kong and Shanghai. In 2021, Adrian moved back to Vancouver with his family, where he met Dariya, his future wife. Adrian is a big watch guy. In his spare time, he loves wine tasting and spending 2 hours pooping in the bathroom.
